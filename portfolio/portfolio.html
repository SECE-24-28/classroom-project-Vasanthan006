PEER TUTORING SCHEDULER

The Peer Tutoring Scheduler is a simple and efficient system designed to connect students with peer tutors. It allows students to book tutoring sessions based on subjects, availability, and time preferences. The system helps tutors manage their schedules, track booked slots, and update their availability easily. It reduces confusion by organizing all tutoring sessions in one centralized platform. Students can view tutor profiles, subjects handled, and select a convenient time for learning. The scheduler improves communication between tutors and learners by providing clear booking details. It ensures that no two students book the same slot, preventing scheduling conflicts. Tutors receive instant updates whenever a new session is booked or modified. The system promotes collaborative learning by making academic support easily accessible. Overall, the Peer Tutoring Scheduler helps create a structured, user-friendly, and effective learning environment.
